Early Pregnancy Awareness

Early pregnancy can have serious health, social, and economic consequences. Understanding its causes and effects can help in prevention.

Causes

  • Lack of proper sex education
  • Peer pressure
  • Sexual abuse
  • Limited access to contraceptives
Learn more

Consequences

  • Health risks for mother and baby
  • Interrupted education
  • Financial difficulties
  • Social stigma
Learn more

Prevention

  • Access to reproductive health education
  • Parental guidance and support
  • Use of contraception
  • Encouraging career and life goals
Learn more

STIs & HIV/AIDS Awareness

Sexually transmitted infections (STIs), including HIV/AIDS, pose serious health risks. Understanding their causes, symptoms, and prevention can help reduce their spread.

Common STIs

  • Chlamydia
  • Gonorrhea
  • Syphilis
  • Herpes
  • HIV/AIDS
Learn more

Symptoms

  • Painful urination
  • Unusual discharge
  • Sores or blisters
  • Itching or irritation
  • Fatigue and fever (HIV/AIDS)
Learn more

Prevention

  • Practicing safe sex (using condoms)
  • Getting regular health check-ups
  • Vaccination (e.g., HPV, Hepatitis B)
  • Being faithful to one partner
  • Educating yourself and others
Learn more

Living with HIV/AIDS

With proper medical care, people living with HIV/AIDS can lead long and healthy lives. Early detection and treatment improve outcomes.
